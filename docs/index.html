How it works

From a touch in VR to a buzz in the room

A small constellation of threads passes one signal down a clean, layered pipeline — discovered automatically, routed in real time.

Your avatar moves

VRChat emits OSC parameters — contacts, proximity, SPS — as you're touched, petted or poked.

The Eardrum vrchat_osc.py

Finds VRChat over mDNS and pulls the full parameter list via OSCQuery. No IP or port typing, ever.

The Brain parameter_store.py

One thread-safe source of truth. A version counter lets routers skip unchanged ticks instantly.

Stateless routers

Pure per-tick math reads the Brain and decides what each device should do — zones, filters, blending, smoothing.

Sealed engines

Each backend is a black box with a primitive-only facade. Add hardware by adding files, never rewiring the old ones.

Route to anything that buzzes, zaps or taps

Mix and match. Bind one avatar zone to many devices, or many zones to one motor — toys, e-stim and suits side by side.

Bluetooth toys

Buttplug.io · Intiface

Auto-discovery, multi-zone routing, per-motor speed-blend tuning and linear-actuator support (Solace Pro, Gravity, OSR2…). Runs a bundled engine — no separate launch needed.

0%

SteamVR trackers

Tundra · Vive · TriggerHapticPulse

Dispatches OSC values to any SteamVR device with per-tracker patterns, plus a battery broadcaster that publishes tracker battery back into VRChat.

0%

bHaptics suits

Vests · arms · feet

Translates v1 bHapticsOSC parameters into dot-mode frames for the bHaptics Player, with an antistuck timer, per-position enables and a live click-to-test dot grid.

0%

PiShock

Shock · vibrate · beep

Fire PiShock shock, vibrate or beep events straight from avatar contacts — over USB serial or the PiShock cloud. Every pulse is hard-capped in the engine, so settings can only ever lower the limits, never raise them.

🛡 Two-layer safety caps
0%

DG-Lab Coyote

Coyote 3.0 · e-stim · BLE

Drives a Coyote 3.0 e-stim unit directly over Bluetooth — no phone app, just a BLE adapter. Independent A/B channel routing, with strength ceilings enforced on the device itself.

🛡 Device-enforced limits
0%

OWO suit

10 muscle groups · EMS

Maps avatar contacts onto OWO's ten muscle-group sensations for full-torso feedback. Pairs with the My OWO phone app over Wi-Fi through the OWO SDK.

0%

Bonus: SteamVR Toy Driver

An optional OpenVR driver that makes your toys & bHaptics positions appear as virtual trackers in SteamVR's device strip — icons and battery included.
